About Us

UNARVU TRIBAL YOUTH CENTRE & LIBRARY

Unarv Tribal Youth Centre and Library was started in November 2016. Special PSC coaching classes and motivation classes are given to tribal youth from various settlements. Apart from this residential PSC coaching classes are also conducted for youths around the Park. Applications and other processes are done at Unarv without charging any fee. Unarv also conducts cultural activities, driving classes, Football fest etc.

LIBRARY

There are over 4000 books available in the library operating at Unarvu.

All sorts of books from stories to autobiographies to scientific literature are available in the library. Students in the area regularly use the facility. Since no other library is operating in the nearby area, the library at Unarv has proven to be a huge success. About 30 magazines and 8 newspapers are available in the Library...
